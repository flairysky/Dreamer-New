Vision & Team

Dreamer Education is built to champion bold applicants with a clear plan, global insight, and human support.

"At critical moments in time you can rise the aspirations of other people significantly, especially when they are relatively young, simply by suggesting they do something better or more ambituous than what they might have in mind. It costs you relatively little to do this but the benefit to them and to the broader world may be enormous. This is in fact one of the most valuable things you can do with your time and with your life."

- Tyler Cowen


Dreamer Education was established to empower curious students, allowing them to unlock their full academic potential by providing comprehensive support and guidance, both in person and through technological tools. This supports students to become the learners of tomorrow: capable of acquiring knowledge efficiently through diverse methods and, more importantly, synthesizing it in novel and unforeseen ways. We are hopeful that such abilities will not only lead to new scientific discoveries but also enable meaningful contributions to everyday applications in their chosen fields. Finally, the ultimate goal is to equip students with all the tools necessary to choose any topic they wish to pursue, and to do so more efficiently, meaningfully, and excitingly than ever before. Such capabilities should also enable them to switch between different subjects based on their evolving interests and preferences in both STEM and non-STEM domains, so that the distinction between the two becomes increasingly blurred, ultimately disappearing entirely.

About Our Team

We are a small team of passionate individuals with diverse academic backgrounds. Our mentors received offers from top ranked universities across Europe and the United States, including for example Columbia University in the City of New York (Ivy League), New York University (NYU), London School of Economics (LSE), Trinity College of Dublin, University College London (UCL), ETH Zurich, University of St. Gallen and many more. What brings us together is a shared passion for learning and a strong belief in the transformative power of the right education. We are committed to helping students navigate their academic journeys with confidence and clarity, providing personalized guidance and support every step of the way.
