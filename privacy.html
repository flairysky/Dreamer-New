Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 4, 2026

Disclaimer: This webpage is intended solely for demonstration purposes; therefore, all information presented herein should not be considered accurate or reliable.

1. Introduction

Dreamer Education ("we," "us," or "our") is committed to protecting the privacy of students, parents, educators, and school representatives using our services. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, when we share it, and your rights regarding your personal information.

2. Scope and consent

This policy applies to all website visitors, registered users of our academic guidance services, and users of our web platform (for example, students, teachers, administrators). By accessing or using our services, you agree to this policy and our Terms of Use.

3. What we collect

a. Information you provide

  • Account and profile data: name, email, role (student/teacher/admin), school affiliation, grade level.
  • Academic data: homework submissions, test scores, assignments, feedback, and other education-related information.
  • Contact data: messages, forum posts, session bookings, and any optional information you share.

b. Automatically collected data

  • Usage information: IP addresses, browser type, device type, pages visited, and time spent.
  • Cookies and tracking: used for login persistence, analytics, and personalization.

4. How we use your data

  • To deliver and maintain our services.
  • To improve usability and personalize learning experiences.
  • To send notifications and provide support.
  • To conduct analytics and comply with legal obligations.

5. Sharing and disclosure of information

  • We do not sell your personal information.
  • Data may be shared with trusted service providers strictly for operational purposes.
  • School-affiliated users may have their information accessed by school administrators.
  • We may disclose data if required by law.

6. Data storage and retention

Your data is securely stored. We retain it for the duration of your account and a reasonable period afterward. You may request deletion at any time.

7. Your rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, or restrict use of your data. Contact us at privacy@dreamereducation.com.

8. Cookies and tracking tools

We use cookies for core functionality and optional analytics. You can manage cookie preferences via browser settings.

9. Updates to this policy

We may update this policy periodically. Please review the "Last updated" date to stay informed.

To get in touch with us, please use our contact form.
