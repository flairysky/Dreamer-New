Guidance that fits your path

When it comes to studying at university, there are many decisions to make. These are often made without much thought, following what your peers are doing or what your surroundings (such as parents and close friends) tell you to do. However, decisions around your studies often determine your life path, and because there are many ways to follow your dreams, we strongly believe that the right mentor can make your studies into a life-changing, enjoyable, and enriching experience. Much like a great teacher can completely transform your understanding of a subject and make you fall in love with it.

Our mentors help you answer questions such as:

  • What subject(s) should I study?
  • Where should I study?
  • How should I structure my studies?
  • What extracurricular activities are there?
  • And many more.

How it works

While every mentorship journey is unique and personalized, the general process looks roughly like this:
  • You apply by filling the form below.
  • If you get selected, we arrange a meeting with a mentor, either online or in person at the ETH Student Project House in Zurich.
  • During the first meeting we will discuss your goals and create a first draft of a personalized plan.
  • Subsequent meetings will help you refine your plan, work on your goals, and track your progress.
